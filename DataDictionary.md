# Data Dictionary

## Fraud

- `Year`: The year the data pertains to

- `All Benefits Fraud`: Total benefits fraud across all programs in dollars or as a percent of total expenditures 

- `[Program Name] Fraud`: Benefits fraud for a specific program in dollars or as a percent of total expenditures

- `[Program Name] Claimant Error`: Error on account of the claimant for a specific program in dollars or as a percent of total expenditures

- `[Program Name] Official Error`: Error on account of the DWP for a specific program in dollars or as a percent of total expenditures

- `[Program Name] Total`:  Total error and fraud for a specific program in dollars or as a percent of total expenditures 

- `[Program Name] Expenditure`: Total government expenditures for a specific program in dollars (For fraud data in dollars only)

## Labor

- `period`: Three month rolling period that the data pertains to

- `16+ Population`: Total number of people age 16+ in the UK

- `16-64 Population`: Total number of people age 16-64 in the UK

- `[Age Range] Total Activity Level`: Total number of people in the age range that are employed or unemployed but seeking work

- `[Age Range] Employment Level`: Total number of people in the age range that are employed

- `[Age Range] Unemployment Level`: Total number of people in the age range that are unemployed

- `[Age Range] Economically Inactive Level`: Total number of people in the age range not employed and are not seeking work.

- `[Age Range] Economic Activity Rate`: The activity level of the age range as a percent

- `[Age Range] Employment Rate`: The employment level of the age range as a percent

- `[Age Range] Unemployment Rate`: The unemployment level of the age range as a percent

- `[Age Range] Economic Inactivity Rate`: The inactivity level of the age range as a percent
