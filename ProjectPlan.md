# Overview

The goal of this project is to analyze UK benefits fraud data along side UK labor data to observe any trends between the labor market and benefits fraud. We plan to compare labor statistics such as unemployment and pay levels over time with benefits fraud levels across different demographics such as gender and age.

# Datasets

This is the official government data on benefits fraud and is available under the [Open Government Licence](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/).
